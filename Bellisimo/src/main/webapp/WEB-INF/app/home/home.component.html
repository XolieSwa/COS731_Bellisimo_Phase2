Bellisimo Hatfield

Hatfield is a suburb in Pretoria, South Africa with a high density of students and student accommodation due its proximity to the University of Pretoria's main campus. Hatfield is home for one of the major campuses of University of Pretoria. Hatfield also houses one of the main stations for the Gautrain, South Africa's premiere mass rapid transit railway. It is home to multiple , popular food and clothing outlets. This website provides you with the details of these outlets and their latest promotions.
